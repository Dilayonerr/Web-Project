Discover Istanbul as a Student

UniGuide Istanbul helps university students who are new to the city find affordable places, cultural spots and student-friendly daily routes.

Explore Routes

Why UniGuide?

  • Budget-friendly cafés, libraries, parks and museums
  • Simple one-day city routes (Kadıköy, Beşiktaş, Taksim…)
  • Tips from a student perspective, not tourists

Our Purpose

Our main goal is to help first-year and international students adapt to Istanbul easily. We provide categorized recommendations, ready-to-use routes and useful information for daily student life.
