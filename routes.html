Kadıköy One-Day Route

Area: Kadıköy • Duration: ~6 hours • Budget: Low

  1. Start with breakfast around Kadıköy Çarşı.
  2. Walk through the streets full of bookstores and vintage shops.
  3. Study or chill at a student-friendly café in Moda.
  4. End the day with sunset at Moda seaside and street food.

Beşiktaş Campus & Bosphorus Route

Area: Beşiktaş • Duration: ~5 hours • Budget: Medium

  1. Visit university campus area around Beşiktaş square.

Taksim & Galata Lite Route

Area: Taksim • Duration: ~5 hours • Budget: Medium

  1. Start at Taksim Square and walk down İstiklal Street.
  2. Stop by bookstores and art galleries on the way.
  3. Reach Galata Tower area and explore side streets.
